Welcome to Show RSS Mobile on Github

This is a native android interface to the fantastic showRSS website. This is an unoffical 3rd party app, the developers have no association with the showrss.kamorra.info website.

showRSS is a service that allows you to keep track of your favorite TV shows. You pick your favorite shows from the list, then you subscribe to your feed. You can subscribe with your torrent client, so latest episodes are automatically downloaded, or add the feed to your browser or feed reader if you want to keep track of latest torrents. Other features also include tracking of your show schedule, so you know exactly when a new episode will air.
